Tekla Auto-Modeler
Automation plugin for Tekla Structures that generates repetitive steel connection details and member layouts using configurable rules, reducing manual modeling time by up to 40%.
Demo video
Project overview

Tekla Auto-Modeler is a custom extension built on top of the Tekla Open API. It allows engineers and detailers to define reusable connection templates and automatically apply them across large models based on rules such as member type, size, and location.

The tool is designed for teams working on repetitive industrial and commercial steel projects, where consistency and speed are critical.

Key features
  • Rule-based placement of steel connections, bracings, and stiffeners directly inside Tekla Structures.
  • Configurable templates that can be saved, shared, and versioned across projects and teams.
  • Interactive preview of affected members before applying bulk changes.
  • Automatic generation of basic GA and assembly drawings for updated members.
  • Logging and reporting of all changes for QA and model auditing.

Use cases

Ideal for steel fabricators, detailers, and engineering teams who need to standardize repetitive modeling tasks while keeping full control of final output.

Can be adapted for company-specific connection libraries, detailing standards, and QA workflows.
